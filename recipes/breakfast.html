Breakfast Recipe

Description

The best breakfast for starting the day off right

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • 4 Bacon strips
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp kethcup
  • 2 Slices of sourdough bread

Recipe

  1. Add 2 Tbsp of olive oil to frying pan. Heat on medium high heat for 2 minutes
  2. Cut bacon strips in half and add to frying pan. Cook for 4-6 minutes or until crispy. Remove from pan leaving oil in.
  3. Add eggs to frying pan. Cook for 4-6 minutes or until white is solid. Flip each and cook for 30 seconds. Remove from pan.
  4. Add ketchup to plate and enjoy with bread.
